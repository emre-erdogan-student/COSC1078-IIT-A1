Background Information

Hello everyone! Welcome to my first ever page. I'll be talking about myself, so you can get to know me better and the type of person I am. As previously mentioned, my name is Emre Erdogan, which has a meaning (surprisingly enough), and it means someone who is a loyal friend in my language. My nationality is Australian, and my cultural background is a mix of Turkish and Kurdish. Even though I am part Kurdish, I don't know how to speak it at all; however, when it comes to Turkish, I can speak it at a very high level.

I am studying ISYS (Information Systems) and am currently in my last year! Which I am very excited about and can't wait to graduate. Before I got into ISYS, I did a diploma in International Business. However, that course didn't really interest me, and a mix of IT and Business was more of something that I wanted to do in the future.


Here are some interesting facts about me:
